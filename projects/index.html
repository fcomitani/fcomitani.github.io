projects

Some of the projects I've been working on

  • Transcriptional heterogeneity of pediatric tumours

    Quantification and characterization of the relationship between intra- and inter-tumour gene expression heterogeneity across a large pan-cancer pediatric cohort.
    Coming soon...

  • FFPE gene expression denoising

    We are building a family of tools to fix the RNA expression profiles degradation caused by formalin-fixation paraffin-embedding and increase its compatibility with data snap-frozen samples.
    Coming soon...

  • Identification of benign and malignant thyroid nodules

    We are investigating transcriptional differences between benign and malignant thyroid neoplasms to improve on current gene expression-based classifiers

past projects

A few of the projects I've worked on in the past

  • Hunting cryptic binding pockets with enhanced sampling

    We improved on the search for allosteric binding sites in target proteins of pharmaceutical interest in molecular simulations by combining fragment probes with a Hamiltonian replica exchange method to enhance the sampling of hydrophobic cavities.

  • How to disrupt the binding properties of ligand-gated ion channels with mutagenesis

    We investigated the binding properties of ligand-gated ion channels with molecular simulations and enhanced sampling methods. The limits of binding interactions were tested through mutagenesis models.

  • The conformational landscape of aspartic acid

    Enhanced sampling simulations allowed us to obtain a complete free energy conformational map for a simple aspartic acid molecule under different environmental conditions.
